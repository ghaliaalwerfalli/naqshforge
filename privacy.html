Orba is a completely offline app. Your financial data never leaves your device, never touches a server, and is never seen by anyone at Naqsh Forge. This policy exists to make that promise official and to satisfy Apple's App Store requirements.

01

Information we collect

None. Orba does not collect any personal information from you. This is not a simplified statement — it is a technical fact about how the app is built.

Orba has no network calls, no analytics SDK, no crash reporting service, and no backend infrastructure. The app operates entirely on your device. When you log an expense, categorise a purchase, or set a budget, that information is written to your device's local storage only — specifically to Apple's AsyncStorage on your iPhone. It goes nowhere else.

Technical fact
Orba makes zero outbound network requests during normal operation. There are no servers receiving your data because there are no servers. The app functions completely without an internet connection, and this is by design — not a limitation.
02

Data stored on your device

The following information is stored locally on your iPhone and is never transmitted anywhere:

Data type What it contains Stored locally Transmitted
Expense entries Amount, category, note, date, time On device only Never
Budget settings Monthly budget amount On device only Never
Currency preference Selected currency code On device only Never
Onboarding status Whether setup is complete On device only Never

All of this data is stored using Apple's standard AsyncStorage API on your device. Deleting the app from your iPhone permanently removes all of this data. There is no cloud backup, no account to restore from, and no way to recover it from Naqsh Forge — because we have never had it.

03

What we never do

The following practices are technically impossible in the current version of Orba, because the infrastructure to do them does not exist:

  • Collect personal information — no name, email, phone number, location, device ID, or any identifier is collected.
  • Connect to your bank — Orba requires no bank connection, banking credentials, or financial account access of any kind.
  • Use analytics tools — no Firebase Analytics, Amplitude, Mixpanel, or any other analytics or behavioural tracking service is embedded in the app.
  • Serve advertisements — Orba contains no advertising SDK, no ad network integration, and no mechanism to display targeted or non-targeted ads.
  • Use crash reporting services — no Sentry, Crashlytics, or similar service is used. Crash information stays on your device.
  • Track your location — Orba never requests location permission and has no access to your GPS or location data.
  • Share data with third parties — we have no data to share, and we have made no agreements to share data with any company.
  • Process payments in-app — purchases are handled entirely by Apple's App Store infrastructure. Naqsh Forge never sees or processes your payment details.
04

Apple App Store purchases

Orba is a paid app sold through the Apple App Store. When you purchase Orba, the transaction is processed entirely by Apple Inc. under Apple's own privacy policy and terms of service.

Naqsh Forge receives payment proceeds from Apple but does not receive your payment card details, Apple ID, or any payment-related personal information. Apple's handling of your purchase is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy, available at apple.com/legal/privacy.

Apple may collect standard App Store usage data (such as download counts and region information) in aggregate form as part of their platform operations. This is governed by Apple, not Naqsh Forge.

05

Children's privacy

Orba is rated 4+ on the App Store and does not contain content restricted to any age group. Because we collect no personal information from any user, we also collect no information from children under 13 (COPPA) or children under 16 (GDPR). The app is safe for use by anyone.

06

Deleting your data

Because all data is stored exclusively on your device, you have complete control over it at all times:

  • Delete the app from your iPhone to permanently remove all Orba data from your device.
  • Reset app data within Orba (Settings → Reset) to clear all entries while keeping the app installed.
  • No request to us is needed — since we hold no data, there is nothing for us to delete on your behalf.

There is no account deletion process because there is no account. When data is deleted from your device, it is gone permanently — there is no server-side backup to restore from.

07

Changes to this policy

If Orba ever adds features that involve data collection — such as optional cloud backup or cross-device sync — this privacy policy will be updated before those features are released. The updated policy will be published at this URL, and the effective date at the top of this page will be updated accordingly.

We will not introduce any data collection without updating this policy first and making it clearly visible in the App Store listing.

08

Applicable law

Naqsh Forge is registered in the United Arab Emirates. This privacy policy is written in compliance with:

  • UAE Federal Decree-Law No. 45 of 2021 on the Protection of Personal Data (PDPL)
  • Apple App Store Review Guidelines — Section 5 (Privacy)
  • GDPR (EU General Data Protection Regulation) — applicable to EU users where relevant
  • COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) — applicable to US users

Because Orba collects no personal data, compliance with all of the above is straightforward: there is no data to protect, process, share, retain, or delete.
